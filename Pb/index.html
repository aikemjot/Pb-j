Minecraft Cake Recipe

Learn how to make a simple Minecraft Cake

Ingredients

  • 3 milk buckets
  • 1 egg
  • 2 sugar
  • 3 wheat

Instructions

  1. Place 3 milk on the top 3 squares
  2. then 3 wheat on the bottom 3 squares
  3. place 2 sugar on the left and right ends
  4. then place the egg in the middle
